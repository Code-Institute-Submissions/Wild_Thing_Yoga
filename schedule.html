Schedule

Yoga has many lineages and we offer a small sample of these. Vinyasa Yoga is an active practice where one posture flows into the next. Hatha Yoga has a flow but is focused on building strength and is based mostly seated or lying on the mat. Yin Yoga classes are slower paced and have a focus on meditaion and restoring your energy. Childrens and Teen classes are a combination of all our offerings with some games/songs for kids and partner/team yoga for our teens. Kids classes are ages 5-11years, Teen 12-16years, Adults 17years +. Level 1 is beginners, 2 Intermediate and 3 Advanced, if there is no level specified this is a mixed ability class.
